OUR SERVICES

Our Recently
Completed Services

Backed by tube mill, cutting, bending, pressing, welding, and in-house inspection, we deliver exhaust and stainless-steel components for automotive, off-road, agri, and power sector applications.

Cut Piece Samples

Precision cut samples prepared in-house through tube cutting operations to support validation, fitment checks, and repeat production.

Corrugated Pipes

Flexible corrugated pipes manufactured for exhaust routing, vibration absorption, and dependable performance in demanding applications.

Bend Pipes

Industrial SS Pipes

Industrial stainless-steel pipes supplied in grades from 202 to 444, conforming to ASTM and international standards.

Tubular Parts

Silencer Assemblies

Stamping Parts - Shell & End Caps
